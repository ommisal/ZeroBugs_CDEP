Processing

Plastic-Bottles & Metal Cans

We will recycle plastic bottles into the polyster threads and that recycled polyster threads are replaced in shoes, clothes, mats and many other stuffs. Plastic bottles can fit in construction also using they filled with fillers like mud, sand and then paste with mixture of clay, sawdust and little amount of cement. Metal cans will be used as raw materials for making new cans and from this the more requirement of metals will be reduced.

Paper and Cardboards waste

Papers and Cardboards are recycle by shredding and pulping and other different steps involves to make new materials. We will send this new materials to various business outlets or manufacturers for manufacturing purposes like newspaper printing ,Wrapping papers , printing papers and blown in cellulose insulators where the recycled papers are used.

Glass-Waste

We will collect the glass waste and then by doing processes like melting ,crushing , cutting theglass waste to make new products which we are going to divided in subcategories according glass quality such as new glass, sand from glass crushing ,and bricks by cutting them into equals parts which are used for construction purposes. The new products which we are going to make are divided in subcategories. This helps to maintain circular economy as making products using recycled glass costs less than making totally new glass products.

General Plastic-Waste

We separate all the general plastic waste into thin plastic and hard plastic. General plastic waste can be used in efficient ways to make plastic bricks and plastic fuel. Likewise plastic bottles, general plastic waste also be turned into useful construction. As the general plastic which we used for making plastic fuel have high calorific value and this fuel is much more efficient and environment friendly which doesn’t makes user compromise with the output.

Cloth-Waste

Unlike other types of waste we collect the cloth waste for only basic need for processing of cloth waste is to make it available in good conditions.By manual segregation any kinds of unwanted materials are removed and then it is put into the system where it is made dirtfree and clean.After the processing it is provided to the local businesses who recycle the waste to make mats, clothes and other stuff from the cloth.
